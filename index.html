Web hosting is the service that allows your website to be accessible on the internet. It involves storing your website's files, data, and content on servers, ensuring they are available for users to access. Reliable web hosting ensures your site's uptime, speed, and security. Choosing the right web hosting provider is vital for a seamless online presence and optimal user experience.

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the practice of optimizing your website to improve its visibility on search engines like Google. It involves techniques such as keyword research, content optimization, and building quality backlinks. SEO is crucial as it enhances organic search traffic, boosts website credibility, and helps your content reach the right audience, ultimately driving online success and business growth.
